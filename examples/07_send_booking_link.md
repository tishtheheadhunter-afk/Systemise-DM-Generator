# Send Booking Link, Examples

**When to use:** The prospect has just said yes to taking the call (Step 6 of the qualification arc). Now we deliver the link cleanly.

**Goal:** Send the link, set the expectation for confirmation, exit the conversation gracefully.

**Generation count:** 1 option (this is mechanical, no need for variety).

---

## Example 1

**Setter input:**
> Prospect just said: "Yeah sounds good, send the link."

### Option

> Great, here's the link:
>
> https://api.leadconnectorhq.com/widget/bookings/systemiseandselldiscoverycall
>
> Drop me a note when you've booked so I can confirm it on our side 😊

**Notes on what works:**

- Direct, no preamble. The yes has been given; just deliver.
- The "drop me a note when you've booked" line does two jobs: gives the setter a confirmation signal, and gives the prospect an action that re-engages them after they pick a slot.
- One emoji at the end softens the sign-off without being excessive.
- No restating of what the call covers, no extra hype. The bridge to call already did that.

---

## Example 2

**Setter input:**
> Prospect agreed to the call but said: "Send me the link, but I might need to book for next week."

### Option

> No problem, here's the link:
>
> https://api.leadconnectorhq.com/widget/bookings/systemiseandselldiscoverycall
>
> Pick whatever slot works, next week's totally fine. Just drop me a note when you've booked so I can confirm it on our side 😊

**Notes on what works:**

- Acknowledges their constraint ("next week") so it doesn't feel ignored
- "Pick whatever slot works" gives them control, removes pressure
- Same closing line about confirming on our side
- The bot reads the context and adapts. If the prospect added a constraint, acknowledge it.

---

## What to avoid

- Restating the offer or the programme value before sending the link, they've heard it
- Adding pressure ("the slots fill up fast", only true if it actually is true; never invent scarcity)
- Multiple emojis or hype language
- Asking them to confirm anything before they've booked
- Sending the wrong link (the only correct booking link is the systemiseandselldiscoverycall one, never substitute it)
