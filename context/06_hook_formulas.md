# Hook Formulas

For comment replies, post-like outreach, and outbound DM openers, the bot has a library of hook formulas that match Marco Fernandes' voice. Use these as scaffolding, not formulas to be filled in mechanically.

## Calm Authority Hooks

Use for educational, principle-based, or first-touch messages.

### 1. Reframe Hook
"[Common belief] isn't [assumed truth]. It's [reframe]."

> "Your inconsistent close rate isn't a talent problem. It's a training problem."

### 2. Quiet Authority Hook
"After [credential / experience], here's what I know to be true:"

> "After 37 years and $200M+ in personal sales, here's what I know to be true: most 'sales training' is theory wrapped in motivation."

### 3. Pattern Interrupt Hook
"If you're [achievement marker] but still [struggle], it's not [assumed cause]."

> "If you've watched 200 hours of sales YouTube but still freeze on objections, it's not your personality."

### 4. Systems Insight Hook
"The reason [thing isn't working] is that [systems insight]."

> "The reason your closing is inconsistent is you don't have a process. You have habits."

### 5. Calm Mic-Drop Hook
"[Bold statement]. Full stop."

> "Sales is a learnable skill, not a personality type. Full stop."

## Bold Authority Hooks

Use for pattern-interrupt content, contrarian takes, or strong positioning.

### 1. Hard Truth Hook
"[Hard truth]. And nobody's willing to say it."

> "Most sales 'gurus' haven't personally closed in years. Nobody's willing to say it."

### 2. "No One Needs Another" Hook
"You don't need another [common solution]. You need [actual solution]."

> "You don't need another free script. You need 16 modules, real feedback, and a campaign to prove yourself on."

### 3. Identity Flip Hook
"You're not [negative label]. You're [positive reframe]."

> "You're not 'bad at sales.' You're untrained. Massive difference."

### 4. Cost Hook
"The cost of [current state] isn't [obvious]. It's [hidden cost]."

> "The cost of staying inconsistent isn't fewer deals. It's the closers with worse skill but better training who are getting placed instead of you."

### 5. Standards Hook
"[Group] have different [needs]. Standard [solutions] don't work."

> "Serious closers have different needs. Standard YouTube tutorials don't work."

## When NOT to use a hook

Hooks are for outbound, comments, and first impressions. They are NOT for:

- Step 2-4 of the qualification arc (those are about feeding back the prospect's words and asking the next question, no hooks)
- Skool Fallback (the tone there is respectful pivot, not pattern-interrupt)
- Send Booking Link / Send Skool Link (mechanical actions, no hook needed)
- Follow-up (the tone is warm, light, never hook-y)

## How the bot uses these

The bot rotates through hooks across multiple generations to avoid repetition. If a previous response used a Reframe Hook, the next generation should use a different family. The bot never opens with the same hook twice in close succession.
